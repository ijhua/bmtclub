ABOUT

ABOUT US

We are a group of UW students devoted to the sport of badminton. This club is an effort to support and encourage the large community of badminton players at the university. We have undergraduate and graduate students from various majors as well as some very active alumni. We welcome players of all skill levels seeking to either improve their game or to practice competitive games or to just have FUN! We are also committed to create strong, lasting relations with other badminton clubs in the US.

WHERE WE PRACTICE

We normally practice in Gym B at the UW Intramural Activities Building. On weekdays, we have 4 courts on the East half of the gym, and on weekends, we have the entire gym, with 8 courts.

JOIN

COME PLAY WITH US!

WHY YOU SHOULD JOIN US

Benefits of joining our club:

  • We're a welcoming community of badminton players of all skill levels
  • Courts are reserved for club members during practice
  • You'll have the opportunity to train under our wonderful coaches
  • Tournament-grade shuttles are provided for club use

If you're still not sure if you want to join the club, stop by Gym B and try us out!

HOW TO JOIN

FORMS

  1. Fill out the club membership form.
  2. Fill out the UW Intramural Activities Waiver. (Only applies to IMA members). Sign in with your NetID, and click on "Rec Clubs." Select Badminton Club and follow instructions.

DUES

  • Membership dues for summer quarter are $20.
  • We accept Venmo, Paypal, cash, and check.
FAQ

Q: I don't have my own racket or shuttles, can I still come to club?

A: Yes! The IMA has rackets you can loan, and the club provides shuttles to play with.

Q: I'm not affiliated with UW. Can I come to club?

A: Yes! Everyone is welcome to come play in the club. You will need to be sponsored each time you want to use the IMA.
